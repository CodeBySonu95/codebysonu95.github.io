Privacy Policy for VoxSherpa

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VoxSherpa. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the VoxSherpa Android application. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

VoxSherpa does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, address, or the text and audio content you create within the app.

All text-to-speech generation and voice processing are performed locally on your device whenever supported by the selected model.

However, certain third-party services integrated into the app may automatically collect limited technical information, diagnostic data, device identifiers, or usage statistics as described in their own privacy policies.

3. Internet and Network Access

VoxSherpa may use an internet connection for the following purposes:

  • Retrieving the latest list of available AI models.
  • Downloading AI model files and updates from Google Firebase.
  • Displaying advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Verifying purchases and subscriptions through Google Play Billing.

Once supported models are downloaded, speech generation can operate fully offline on your device.

4. Local Storage

VoxSherpa stores downloaded AI models, generated audio files, application settings, and related data locally on your device.

These files remain under your control and are not uploaded to our servers.

5. Voice Data and Generated Content

Voice samples, cloned voices, generated audio files, imported text, and other user-created content are processed locally on the device whenever possible.

VoxSherpa does not intentionally collect, upload, or store your voice recordings, cloned voice profiles, generated speech, or imported text on developer-controlled servers.

6. Third-Party Services

VoxSherpa may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing

These services may collect limited information necessary for analytics, diagnostics, advertising, security, fraud prevention, purchase verification, and service functionality.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Advertising

The free version of VoxSherpa may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

AdMob may use device identifiers and other technologies to provide, measure, and improve advertising services. Data collection performed by AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Purchases and Subscriptions

Premium purchases and subscriptions are processed securely through Google Play Billing.

VoxSherpa does not collect, process, or store your payment card information. Payment information is handled directly by Google.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the app and its associated services. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children's Privacy

VoxSherpa is not directed toward children under the age required by applicable laws in their region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as VoxSherpa evolves and new features are introduced.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date on this page.
